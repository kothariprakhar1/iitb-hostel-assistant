HOSTEL 16 RULES & REGULATION

A] DEFACING OF WALLS / DOORS / WINDOWS OF HOSTEL ROOMS
1. Walls, doors, and windows of hostel rooms should not be defaced by the inmates.
2. Do not write with pen, pencil, or paint on walls, doors, and windows.
3. Do not paste posters or photos on the walls/doors. Heavy fine will be imposed if any inmate is found indulging in the aforementioned activities.
4. Furniture should not be changed/exchanged with other rooms/occupants without the knowledge of the Hall Manager. Furniture items should not be put outside the room/corridor.
5. Plywood tops of cots should not be removed/replaced from the frame of cots. Furniture items will be repaired/replaced if found defective due to wear and tear. Heavy fine will be imposed if furniture is damaged due to negligence of the inmate.
6. Furniture items should not be taken out of the room for any purpose/function without written permission from the Warden/Hall Manager. A gate pass has to be obtained from the Security Officer if a furniture item has to be taken out of the hostel after prior permission from the Warden.
7. If a furniture item is found defective, it should be reported to the Hall Manager immediately for repairs/replacement.
8. In case of any damage/breakage of Institute/Hostel property, the cost will be recovered from occupants.

B] ROOM CLEANING SCHEDULE (every Sat/Sun, once a month)
Students are expected to get room cleaning done by the housekeepers in their presence on a regular basis.

C] UNAUTHORISED STAY
Guests/friends/parents/relatives are not allowed to stay overnight in rooms allotted to students. For availing guest room facility, prior permission from the Hostel Coordinating Unit / Warden / Hall Manager has to be obtained. Guest rooms will be allotted, subject to availability, on a payment basis.
Ladies/guests/relatives are allowed to visit the inmates only between 7:30 AM to 10:00 PM after making proper entries in the register available with security personnel on duty.

D] DEFACING/BREAKING IN COMMON PLACES
If any defacing/breakage/damage occurs in common places, the cost will be recovered from all wing inmates in each case, through mess bills. Fan/lights are to be switched off when inmates are out of the room. Minimum fine for this violation is Rs. 500/-.

E] CYCLE PARKING
Residents shall park their bicycles in the designated area only. Violation might lead to confiscation of the cycle and a fine of Rs. 1000/- per violation, paid by the owner. Guests should park their cars/motorcycles/bicycles only in the guest-parking area or outside the hostel premises.

F] CHANGE/EXCHANGE OF ROOMS
Changing/shifting rooms is not allowed without prior permission of the Warden/Hall Manager.

Residents shall not consume alcohol or any intoxicating substance in the hostel premises.
Under no circumstances may students attempt to procure duplicate keys or call locksmiths to the hostel premises without written authorization from the Warden/Hall Manager.
Heavy fine will be imposed and disciplinary actions initiated by the Institute/hostel authorities for breaking of the above rules.
