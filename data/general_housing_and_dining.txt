Topic: Hostel facilities & mess (institute-wide, general)

HOSTELS (General Overview)

IIT Bombay has 17 hostels catering to student accommodation. Hostel No. 1 to 9, and 12, 13, 14, and 15 (A and B wing) are for boys, while Hostel No. 10, 10A, 11, and 15 (C wing) are for girls.

Every hostel has washing and ironing facilities, a computer room, a TV, sports grounds, and indoor games like Table Tennis, Carrom Board, Chess, etc. Access rules vary by hostel, but in general these facilities can be availed against a student's I-Card. There is internet connection in each room.

Several hostels have shops meeting daily needs and requirements, and also provide stationery, xerox, and printing facilities.

Messes are present in each individual hostel, serving food four times a day. There are also several additional food outlets spread around the campus that students may choose to visit.

ROOM RETENTION (General policy applicable to all hostels)

All students currently residing in the hostels must adhere to the following rules regarding room retention during vacations:

1. All students must submit the room retention form, duly signed by the respective authority, by the assigned date - i.e., 25th April for summer vacation, and an announced date for winter vacation.

2. If the retention is not approved, the room should be vacated by the assigned date and the keys handed over to the hall manager. A fine of Rs. 500/day applies in case of non-compliance.
