Information for UG New Entrants (2025-2026)

Key dates:
- Hostel Check-in for students: 20th July 2025 (Sunday), 09:00 am onwards
- Start of Orientation: 21st July 2025 (Monday), 09:00 am (Reporting time: 08:30 am)
- Orientation duration: July 21st - 26th 2025
- Instruction begins: 28th July 2025 (Monday)

Before Coming to the Institute:
- Visit the IT services page to accept the IT Access Policy and activate IT services login ID. The login ID is the Roll Number (in lowercase), with initial password shared via registered email.
- Log in to the ASC portal and complete: fill personal information and accept declarations; pay the Semester Mess Advance (SMA), which is mandatory as soon as the roll number is generated (late fee applies after the deadline); print pre-filled forms (Student and Parent Declaration Form, Hostel Registration Form, Medical File Record) to bring and sign at the registration desk.

Upon Arrival at the Institute:
- Show the barcode on the Visitor Entry Pass at the Main Gate for entry.
- Students occupy hostel rooms starting 20th July 2025, 9:00 am onwards.
- Report at the Common Registration Desk located near Hostel 1 (old SAC parking lot) along with the Hostel Registration Form to complete formalities and collect room keys. Student volunteers guide students to their hostel/room.
- Orientation and course Registration starts 21st July 2025 (Monday).
- ID card generation, original certificate verification, biometric verification, and medical file record happen from 22nd July 2025 to 26th July 2025.

Hospital Related Information:
- During hospital registration, the filled and downloaded medical record form is checked by doctors for recommended vaccination status at IITB Hospital.
- On verification, the doctor authenticates the records and a medical file is created and retained by the student. This file is compulsory for all hospital visits to IITB Hospital, used for: medical consultation; maintaining medical/surgical history; issuing medical certificates for treatments undertaken at IITB hospital; noting previous drug allergies/other allergies; reimbursement of medicine/treatments taken at IITB hospital (for medicines not available in the IIT pharmacy) or at referred empanelled hospitals outside; and knowing the patient's previous medical/surgical history in case of an emergency visit.

Vaccination Information:
- Recommended vaccines: MMR (booster recommended after 15 years of age), Chicken Pox, Typhoid, Hepatitis A.
- Students should take at least one of these vaccines (preferably MMR) before arriving on campus, and bring updated vaccination records for verification. At least proof of one vaccine is required to make a Medical folder.
- Students without a vaccination record may be given the vaccine at IITB hospital on a payment basis.

Checklist of Essential Documents to Bring to Campus:
- Visitor Pass (on phone or printed)
- Declaration form (student and parent/guardian signed)
- Hostel registration form (after SMA payment)
- Medical Record Form with immunisation certificates/records
- Childhood immunisation certificates/records and previous medical records (if any)
- Printout of the seat allotment letter provided by JoSAA/UCEED/INMO
- Originals and a photocopy of all documents/certificates given in the offer letter

Additional facilities/information:
- The Student Mentor Programme website introduces freshers to life at IIT Bombay from a student's perspective.
- All UG entrants are expected to have their own laptops; a recommended laptop configuration is published by the Institute, and loan assistance for IT purchases is available for needy students.
- Anti-discrimination guidelines, Gender Cell guidelines, and Security guidelines are published by the Institute for students' reference.
- Important student support links include the Student Wellness Center, Gender Cell, and SC/ST Cell.
