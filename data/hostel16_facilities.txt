Topic: Hostel facilities

HOSTEL FACILITIES

Basic facilities: 1 bed, 1 chair, 1 study table, and 1 cupboard provided to every student.

Laundry & drinking facilities: 1 washing machine, 1 cloth dryer, and 1 water filter on each floor, shared for 36 students.

Sport facilities: A sport room in A-Wing and B-Wing is available 24 hours. Students can issue sport items 24 hours.

Computer room: Located in C-Wing near the Common Room, with 8 desktop PCs available.

First aid: Available at the Security Desk of A, B, and C Wing.

Items that can be issued from the Hostel Office: DSLR camera, projector, projector screen, sound system, 24-switch LAN box, and sports material.

Other on-site facilities mentioned: hostel mess, hostel canteen, a stationery shop (DHUN), designated cycle parking areas, and an iron & dry cleaning service in A-Wing.
